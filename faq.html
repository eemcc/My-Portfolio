Why do you want to become a developer?

I want to be a part of a team creating something really cool. I have been behind the scenes of a rebrand and a revamp of a company's website. The excitement of creating something new or revitalizing a person, place or thing pushes me to put in the work.

What are your goals in this career?

Right now my goal is to learn as much as possible. I would love to be around many experienced developers that wish to share their knowledge. I want a career where I can be a part of a team that builds something that I can see is making a difference. Eventually, I'd like to be in a position to lead and teach others what I have learned.

What is your past experience in web development?

I took two computer science classes in college. The first was learning basics and in the second one I built a simple game. At my Marketing Internship I helped edit the website during the rebrand. No one in the office knew wordpress, but I was able to use what I learned in school and some research to make the website look how they wanted it to look.
